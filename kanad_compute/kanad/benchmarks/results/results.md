# Kanad Tier-1 Benchmark Results

> Small-molecule Tier-1 (4–16 qubit, STO-3G) results are below. For the
> large-active-space SQD champions on real IBM Heron (naphthalene 32q,
> [Fe₂S₂Cl₄]²⁻ 40q) plus observables/forces/dynamics at scale, see
> [`results_scale.md`](results_scale.md).

Generated by `python -m benchmarks.run_benchmarks`. STO-3G basis, frozen-core where noted.

Chemical accuracy = ≤1.6 mHa from full-FCI. **CASCI** is the active-space Hamiltonian's lowest N-sector eigenvalue (direct ED) — measures whether the Hamiltonian is bit-correct. **VQE** is `mode='standard'` + L-BFGS-B + Givens-SD ansatz (M2.5; particle-conserving, breaks Brillouin via paired doubles).

**M2 done as of M2.7 (analytical gradient):** Givens-SD with brick-wall singles + all opposite-spin doubles + same-spin doubles spans the FCI manifold; M2.6 decomposes to single-Pauli rotations (~50× speedup); M2.7 adds an adjoint-state gradient (∂E/∂θ_k = i⟨ψ|[G_k, H]|ψ⟩, one forward + one backward pass per gradient call). The combined effect is that 8/8 Tier-1 molecules now reach chemical accuracy in 7–11 outer L-BFGS-B iterations each. Total wall ≈24 min on a laptop, under PLAN.md's 30-min budget.

## Per-case notes

- **H2**: 4 qubits; baseline.
- **HeH+**: 4 qubits; charged. Tests M1 D1 IonicHamiltonian fix.
- **LiH**: Freeze Li 1s. (2e, 5o) active.
- **BeH2**: Freeze Be 1s. (4e, 6o); 92 params, max_iter bumped
- **H2O**: Freeze O 1s. The closed-shell chemistry benchmark.
- **HF**: Freeze F 1s. Ionic; end-to-end test of M1 D1.
- **NH3**: Freeze N 1s. C₃ᵥ symmetry; ~200 params, may need extra iters.
- **CH4**: Freeze C 1s. Tᵈ symmetry; ~360 params, hardest Tier-1 case.
